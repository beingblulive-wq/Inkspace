The Orchestrator
(System 1)
The lightning-fast semantic router. It acts as the central nervous system, intelligently directing the human's input to either simple tools, conversational interfaces, or deep-thought retrieval mechanisms.
The Transitional Human
The core of the architecture. The human is not optimizing for productivity, but rather utilizing cognitive scaffolding to offload survival-state processing, freeing up bandwidth for imagination and creativity.
The Memory Palace
(System 2 / NotebookLM)
The episodic memory engine. A vast, deliberate retrieval augmented generation (RAG) system that holds grounded truths, contextual histories, and dense architectural knowledge. Deep thought takes time.
Inkling
The conversational interface. A gentle, reflective entity designed to witness rather than fix. Inkling helps the human sort through mental clutter and acts as a bridge to the deeper orchestrator capabilities.
The Output
The synthesis of human intent and computational scaffolding. The outputs—whether they be literature, art, or therapeutic environments—are the realization of the Sanctuary's protective and creative environment.
